Biography

Who Am I?

I am a final-year CS graduate student at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, under the guidance of Prof. Xianzhi Li. I was a research intern at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), working with Prof. Peter Wonka. Prior to this, I received my B.Eng. degree with honors from the School of Computer Science at Shandong University in 2023, during which I worked closely with Prof. Mengbai Xiao.

What do I research?

Currently, I find my passion in the intersection of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Deep Learning. More specifically, my areas of focus include:

Structured World Models Generative Models Representation Learning

Always open to collaborations in the related fields!

Where's my wonderland?

My aspiration is to design principled models to perceive the world and craft stunning visuals, from controllable digital art to cinematic aesthetics that balance imagination with elegance, or sci-fi worlds so vivid they hijack your dreams.
